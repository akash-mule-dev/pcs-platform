The problem

Running a growing floor on paper? You're flying half-blind.

Paper travelers and spreadsheets work fine with six jobs open. At sixty, they break — and you can't see the floor, the labor, or the quality in time to act.

Stale visibility

You learn where a job stands at the end of the shift — not while you can still do something about it.

Labor you can't trust

Hand-written times are slow and skipped, so costing and quoting become guesswork.

QA that misses the part

A 2D checklist can't verify a built assembly against intent — rework shows up late.
