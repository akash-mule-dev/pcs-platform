What sets us apart

Quality you can see, in 3D and AR.

Most shops check a built assembly against a 2D drawing on a clipboard. FabriXR puts the actual 3D model in the operator's hands — on a tablet or in AR, right at the station — and pins each defect directly to the geometry.

  • Inspect the built assembly against the real 3D model — not a flat checklist.
  • Defects pinned to the geometry: type, severity, measurement, tolerance.
  • AR-assisted inspection right where the work happens.
  • Sign-offs and defect-pattern views build a real quality record.
