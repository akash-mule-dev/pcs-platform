================================================================
  Sales & Marketing — Go-to-Market Plan
================================================================

TARGET MARKET
----------------------------------------------------------------
  Industry: Discrete manufacturing (automotive parts, electronics assembly,
            precision machining, medical devices)
  Company size: SME manufacturers (50–500 employees)
  Geography: India-first (Pune/Maharashtra manufacturing clusters — Chakan,
             Ranjangaon, Hinjewadi MIDC), then expand nationally
  Buyer persona: Plant Head, Production Manager, Quality Manager, CTO/CIO
  Pain points: Paper/Excel tracking, no real-time visibility, manual quality
               inspection, no OEE data, shift handover chaos

CORE DIFFERENTIATORS
----------------------------------------------------------------
  • Real-time per-operator, per-stage time tracking with mobile clock-in
  • 3D/AR quality inspection — color-coded defect visualization on actual models
  • Kanban work order management with dependency tracking
  • OEE dashboard with live production analytics
  • Mobile operator app with offline mode and NFC badge scan
  • WebSocket real-time monitoring — no page refresh needed

PRICING STRATEGY (INR)
----------------------------------------------------------------
  Starter:      ₹15,000–25,000/month   (1 line, ≤20 operators, core features)
  Professional: ₹40,000–75,000/month   (multi-line, 3D/AR, analytics, mobile)
  Enterprise:   Custom                  (multi-tenant, SSO, on-prem, SLA)
  Pilot:        Free 30–60 day trial for first 5 customers (to earn case studies)
